Spaghetti

Description:

Classic Italian meat sauce with noodles.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Spices
    • Oregano
    • Basil
    • Salt
    • Black Pepper
  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until meat is browned and crumbly and vegetables are tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain grease.
  4. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve meat sauce on top of noodles.
